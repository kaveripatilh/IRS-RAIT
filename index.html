About ISA-RAIT

The International Society of Automation's student chapter, ISA-RAIT serves as an instrumental element in developing, strengthening and imparting knowledge of Instrumentation and Automation. It is the best place for a student to develop a fervent interest in the subject and to gain the right guidance and opportunities. Throughout the year an array of events is conducted here, to bring innovation and technology together, while learning as much as possible. Various successful seminars, tech-talks, workshops and our much renowned Technical fest - GENESIS, have always brought laurels to the student body! Our events and publications are much awaited and have always been extremely insightful about the upcoming technologies! ISA - RAIT's annual publications consist of a Technical Newsletter and a Magazine which reflect the ideas, work and vision of the Student body and it's members in a very creative manner.We at ISA-RAIT cherish being the part of a renowned international conglomerate, full of innovative automation enthusiasts! Since our advent in the year 2002, we have always been extremely active.
